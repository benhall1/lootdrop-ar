Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 5, 2026

LootDrop AR ("we", "us", "our") is a location-based coupon discovery app. This policy explains what we collect, how we use it, and your choices.

What we collect

  • Account data: name, email, and (if you sign in with Google or Apple) the basic profile info those providers share.
  • Location: your approximate device location while the app is open, so we can show nearby loot drops and verify proximity when you claim a coupon.
  • Activity: coupons you claim, redeem, and favorite. Aggregated event counts (XP, streaks, badges).
  • Device data: push notification subscription tokens (only if you opt in).

How we use it

  • To show you relevant loot drops within walking distance.
  • To verify you are physically near a merchant when you claim a coupon (anti-fraud).
  • To send push notifications about new drops in your area (only if you opt in).
  • To show merchants aggregate stats (claim counts, redemption rates) — never your individual identity.

What we do NOT do

  • We do not sell your personal data.
  • We do not share your precise location with merchants.
  • We do not track you when the app is closed.

Third parties

We use Supabase for authentication, database, and notifications, and Stripe for merchant payments. These providers process data on our behalf under their own privacy terms.

Your choices

  • Sign out at any time from the Profile screen.
  • Request account deletion by emailing benhallbyu@gmail.com.
  • Disable location or push notifications via your browser/device settings.

Contact

Questions? Email benhallbyu@gmail.com.
